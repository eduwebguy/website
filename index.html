About me

I am currently the Web Content Specialist at Blinn Community College. I have earned an Associate in Applied Science Degree in Computer Information Systems with an emphasis in Web Development from Amarillo College, a Certificate of Completion in Computer Information Systems from Amarillo College, and a Certificate of Completion in Web Development and Design from the University of Texas at Austin Continuing and Extended Education.

I am passionate about learning and sharing my thoughts and ideas to the higher education website community. My focus is not on website design alone, but how a website performs for the user.

My philosophy is to keep things simple for website usability, focus on website standards for website accessibility, and to ensure that higher educational websites meet the highest standards of editorial and graphic quality.
